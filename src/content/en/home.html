Welcome

Hey there! Welcome to my personal website. I'm Frederik, a physiotherapy student at San Pablo University in Madrid, Spain. I love promoting health and helping people achieve their functional goals.

For a large number of people in society, there's one particularly unpleasant sensation and emotion which intrudes on many aspects of their life: pain.

With a focus on pain as both a symptom and a condition, my goal is to provide free resources for clinicians, patients, and other curious people, so that everyone can better understand this misunderstood and stigmatized experience.

Feel free to download and redistribute my content.

Disclaimer: This website is for educational purposes only. I am not providing personalized medical advice, and I take no responsibility for what you do with the information presented.
