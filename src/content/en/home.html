Welcome

Hey there! I'm Frederik, a physiotherapy student at San Pablo University in Madrid, Spain. I love promoting health and helping people achieve their functional goals.

For a large number of people in society, there's a very personal, often unpleasant sensation and emotion which intrudes on many aspects of their life: pain.

With a focus on pain as both a symptom and a condition in and of itself, my goal here is to develop a variety of free resources for clinicians, patients, and other curious people. I want to make it easy for everyone to make sense of this common yet misunderstood (and stigmatized) experience.

Feel free to download and redistribute my content! Just keep in mind that I will be continually updating the website as my views and ambitions evolve.

DISCLAIMER: This website is for educational purposes only. I am not providing personalized medical advice and I take no responsibility for what you do with the information presented.
