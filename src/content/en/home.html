Welcome!

Hey there! I'm Frederik, a physical therapy student at San Pablo University in Madrid, Spain. I love promoting holistic health and empowering people to achieve their functional goals.

For a large number of people in society, there's a very personal, often unpleasant sensation and emotion which intrudes on many aspects of their life: pain.

With a focus on pain as both a symptom and a condition in and of itself, my goal here is to develop a variety of free educational resources for clinicians, patients, and other curious people. I want to make it easy for everyone to make sense of this common yet misunderstood (and stigmatized) experience.
